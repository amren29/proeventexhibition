Who We Are

Creative Minds Behind
Extraordinary Events

Pro Event & Exhibition is a premium creative agency based in Kota Kinabalu, Sabah. We specialize in designing, producing, and managing exhibitions, corporate events, conferences, product launches, brand activations, and custom exhibition booths.

From concept development to final installation, we deliver complete end-to-end event solutions tailored to each client's vision — helping businesses, government agencies, and organizations across East Malaysia create lasting impressions.

With over a decade of experience, we've worked with leading brands, government bodies, universities, and hospitality groups — bringing each project to life with creativity, precision, and professionalism.
